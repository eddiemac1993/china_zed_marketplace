Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers about deposits, delivery, product requests, balance payments, and order changes.

Why is the deposit 35%?

The 35% deposit confirms your order and helps secure the product from the supplier before shipping begins. The balance is paid when the goods arrive and are ready for pickup or delivery.

How long does shipping take?

China pre-orders usually show a 14-30 day delivery window. Some items can take longer because of supplier delays, shipping schedules, customs, or holidays.

Can I send my own product link?

Yes. Use the product request form to send links from Alibaba, Taobao, Temu, 1688, Shein, or another supplier. We will check availability and prepare a Zambia price quote.

What if my item is unavailable?

We confirm availability before final sourcing. If the item is unavailable, we can help find an alternative, adjust the order, or handle the deposit according to the order policy.

When do I pay the balance?

The balance is paid when your China order arrives in Zambia and is ready for pickup or local delivery.

Why must I verify my email?

Email verification prevents fake accounts and helps make sure order updates, activation links, and receipts go to the right customer.
