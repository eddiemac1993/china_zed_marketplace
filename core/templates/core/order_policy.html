📋 Please read and understand how our China-to-Zambia ordering system works before placing an order.
1. Order Confirmation

After placing an order on the website, the customer must confirm the order through WhatsApp. Orders are only processed after manual confirmation to ensure accuracy and availability.

2. Deposit Requirement

A 20% deposit is required to process any order. This confirms commitment and allows us to secure the item from China.

The remaining 80% balance is paid after the goods arrive in Zambia.

3. Refund Policy
  • Deposit is refundable if the item is not delivered within the agreed time.
  • Refund applies if the supplier fails to fulfill the order.
  • Refund applies if the product becomes unavailable before purchase.
4. Delivery Timeline

Each product has an estimated delivery range displayed on the website. Delivery times may vary due to:

  • Shipping delays
  • Customs clearance
  • Supplier processing time
  • Logistics and transport conditions
5. Order Tracking

Customers can log in to their profile to track order progress, including:

  • Confirmed
  • Purchased
  • Shipped
  • In Transit
  • Arrived in Zambia
  • Ready for Collection
  • Successful / Completed
  • Delayed
6. Receipts

Customers can generate a receipt once the goods have arrived in Zambia or when the order is ready for collection or delivery.
